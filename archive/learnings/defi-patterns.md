# DeFi Investment Patterns

## Success Factors

### Strong Governance Decentralization
- **Description**: Protocols with voting power distributed across >100 active delegates tend to be more resilient
- **Supporting Evidence**: Uniswap, Compound, Aave governance participation
- **Confidence**: High
- **Tags**: [governance, decentralization]

### Sustainable Yield Sources
- **Description**: Protocols deriving yield from real economic activity (trading fees, lending interest) vs token emissions
- **Supporting Evidence**: Uniswap fee revenue, Aave interest spread
- **Confidence**: High
- **Tags**: [tokenomics, sustainability]

### Multi-Chain Presence
- **Description**: Protocols deployed across 3+ chains show higher TVL resilience during chain-specific events
- **Supporting Evidence**: Aave, Curve multi-chain deployments
- **Confidence**: Medium
- **Tags**: [infrastructure, diversification]

## Warning Indicators

### Rapid TVL Growth Without Revenue
- **Description**: TVL growth >100% in 30 days without corresponding fee revenue increase
- **Historical Cases**: Terra/Anchor, various yield farms
- **Mitigation**: Compare TVL growth to organic revenue metrics
- **Tags**: [risk, tvl, revenue]

### Concentrated Token Holdings
- **Description**: Top 10 wallets holding >50% of governance tokens
- **Historical Cases**: Various DAO takeover attempts
- **Mitigation**: Monitor holder distribution via on-chain analytics
- **Tags**: [governance, concentration]

## Market Cycle Indicators

### Bull Market Patterns
- TVL typically expands 5-10x from cycle low
- New protocol launches increase significantly
- Retail participation visible in social metrics

### Bear Market Patterns
- TVL contracts to "real" value (fee-generating capacity)
- Protocol consolidation and M&A activity
- Focus shifts to fundamentals and revenue
